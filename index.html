FAQ

Common questions from BD leaders and managing partners.

Is this just a news alert or lead list?
No. We only include items with verifiable PE ownership and a clearly articulated signal under a strict taxonomy. Each item includes evidence, a confidence score, and outreach framing (why now / what angle).
How do you avoid false positives?
We treat false positives as worse than misses. Routine hiring, vague keyword matches, growth announcements, and unverifiable ownership are excluded. Signals must contain explicit contextual indicators beyond generic mentions.
Do you provide contact information?
No. We provide outreach-ready intelligence and role-based guidance (e.g., CFO, Head of Transformation, Ops Lead) where relevant—without selling personal data.
Can you tailor coverage to our niche?
Yes. We can scope by sponsor set, sector, geography, and trigger focus (e.g., carve-outs, integration, leadership change, PMO build-out).
Do you infer distress or predict outcomes?
No. We surface publicly observable signals, cite evidence, and score confidence strictly from sources. We do not diagnose distress, predict outcomes, or infer sponsor intent.
What does “exclusivity” mean?
Exclusivity can be structured at the sponsor level (e.g., exclusive coverage for a specific PE firm) or sector level (e.g., one buyer per vertical), ensuring your outbound advantage isn’t diluted.
